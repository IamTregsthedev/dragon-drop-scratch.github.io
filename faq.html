Do I need any HTML/CSS/JavaScript knowledge to make a website through DragonDrop?

No, you don't need any coding language to use DragonDrop, as DragonDrop is a drag'n'drop website editor. However, if you do know some HTML you can put it in to your website through HTML snippets.

Do I need to pay to have Scratch tools on DragonDrop?

No, you just need a Scratch account. These tools should automatically work for you.

Will the website I create go online the second I create it?

Yes, the website will go online the instance you click that publish button.

Will I have DragonDrop Ads on my website?

You will have a optional small watermark in the corner of your website. As for ads, you can suport us by letting us put ads on your page. You are free to put your own ads up too.

Do I get a free domain?

When creating your site you will get four options. The three free options are to use a ".tk" domain, a ".dragondrops.net" domain, or to transfer a domain you already own over to DragonDrop, or you can buy a domain from us.

I'm confused. Do I have to pay?

The DragonDrop CMS is completely free. However, connecting an external domain, or buying a domain from us will be paid. Although, websites created with Dragon Drop that are subdomains of Dragondrops.net will be free. The default name for your website will be yourtitle.dragondrops.net. More info on domains can be found here.

Do I get a free email?

No, free emails are not provided with the free service given. You must purchase a Premium Service for an email account.
