DragonDrop Widgets

DragonDrop Widgets are tools that you can drop into your website on the website editor. These include text widgets, image widgets, video widgets, and tons of others that we have created to let you put the spesific item into your website.
To put a widget into your website, simply go to the editor and then drag the widget onto you website or click it.
To switch between menus click on the menu header.
